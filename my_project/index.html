The Health Club and Coding Club

What are Chiliblains?

Chilblains are itchy, swollen bumps that appear on the skin when it's exposed to cold and damp conditions. They usually affect the toes, fingers, ears, or nose.

What are the causes?

Chilblains happen when skin is exposed to cold and blood vessels constrict, then expand too quickly in warm conditions, causing inflammation.

What are the prevention tips?

• Keep warm and dry: Layer up, especially on extremities, and avoid dampness.

• Avoid rapid temperature changes: Gradually warm up after being in the cold.

• Protect skin: Wear insulated gloves and socks.

• Boost circulation: Move and stretch your hands and feet regularly.

What are the treatments?

• Gently warm affected areas, but avoid direct heat!

What are the treatments?

• Apply soothing creams or corticosteroids.

What are the treatments?

• If symptoms persist, see a doctor.
